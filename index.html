About Me

Julia Pauletti taught herself to play "Jingle Bells" on the piano when she was three years old and has been singing and involved in music ever since. She was a part of her high school's show choir all four years, while also playing at coffee shops in her spare time.

Julia first attended Belmont University to study Commercial Music and Songwriting before transferring to UT Dallas to study Emerging Media & Communication. While her career led her away from music, she continues to record covers and occasionally releases an EP in her free time. Her latest accomplishment is being able to play (most of) Bohemian Rhapsody by Queen on the piano.
