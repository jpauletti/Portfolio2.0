About Me

Julia Pauletti first experimented with code when Myspace became popular. She enjoyed creating visually appealing layouts.

When it came time to decide on a career, she was unsure of what to do. She ended up pursuing User Experience Design because she thought it was interesting, but she found something she really liked when she began working on a development team.

Whenever she had downtime with her UX duties, she would help the dev team with anything they needed assistance with at the time, and she began to realize that coding was something she really enjoyed. It was like a puzzle.

Julia mostly had experience with basic front-end technology, and so she decided to take a bootcamp to have more well-rounded experience and be able to pursue a full-time job in full stack web development.
